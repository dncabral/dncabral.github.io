“Imagination is more important than knowledge. For knowledge is limited, whereas imagination embraces the entire world, stimulating progress, giving birth to evolution”


Albert Einstein


Assistant Professor at ISEL, Polytechnic University of Lisbon, and Researcher at ITI/LARSyS.


Previously, I was Senior Research Fellow at Interactive Technologies Institute (ITI), LARSyS, IST, University of Lisbon; Assistant Professor at the University of Madeira, where I lectured in the HCI Master program (MHCI), in partnership with HCII, Carnegie Mellon University, for 3 academic years. I was also a postdoctoral researcher at Helsinki Institute for Information Technology (HIIT)/University of Helsinki, working with Prof. Giulio Jacucci in the Ubiquitous Interaction Group (UIx). I have a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Universidade NOVA de Lisboa, Portugal, supervised by Prof. Nuno Correia at the Interactive Multimedia Group (IMG). My doctoral studies were mainly funded by a Ph.D. grant from the UTAustin|Portugal, Digital Media Program.
